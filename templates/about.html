About Us

About Us

Aruvi Infotech, established in 2019, is committed to providing employment opportunities for students enrolled in its Aruvi Institute of Learning. Acknowledging the hurdles faced by freshers, particularly female candidates, in job searches, the company initiated programs to offer practical experience and facilitate placements in reputable companies. By 2024, Aruvi Infotech has successfully developed and published numerous websites, including those for its institute and employment office. Currently, the company proudly employs learners from its institute, each involved in diverse projects, fostering growth and innovation. Specializing in website development, deployment, data analytics, and machine learning, Aruvi Infotech also offers comprehensive documentation for final year projects, provides internships for college students, and conducts workshops in several colleges to further enhance their skills and knowledge.

Our Vision

At Aruvi Infotech, we're dedicated to breaking barriers for aspiring technologists, particularly women, by offering practical experience and strategic placements. Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills and opportunities needed to thrive in the software industry. Join us in shaping a future where talent knows no bounds and innovation flourishes.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to bridge the gap between education and employment. We understand the challenges that freshers, particularly female candidates, face in securing job opportunities. Hence, we have dedicated ourselves to offering practical experience and facilitating placements in renowned companies
